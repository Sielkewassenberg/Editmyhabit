By starting to visualise your habits, you have a clear view of all your routines, habits and daily activities. The visual display increases self-awareness and you clearly notice which areas need improvement and where things are going wrong. So you can easily identify and overcome your obstacles. A planner acts as a motivator and reminder, promoting commitment and accountability. They also provide positive reinforcement by releasing dopamine when you can fill in a box. Which in the long run ensures behavioural change where negative patterns are replaced by positive ones.

A habit tracker is a tool, often in the form of a graph or table, that allows individuals to track and visualise their daily habits. It allows users to track their progress, identify patterns and gain a deeper understanding of their behaviour and routines.
